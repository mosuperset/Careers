Web Developer.

If you choose web development here is what you should know.

Responsiblities and Duties.

As a Web Developer, you will markup, style, deploy and edit webpages.

you will need to meet with your clients/employer to discuss their needs and present solutions.

Being a web developer requires more than being proficient in programming. You will also be expected to work as part of a team, which means you must be a good communicator. Problem-solving is the heart of the industry. As the field of web development is still young and thriving, some problems are yet to be solved, and most have multiple solutions. Picking the right solution for the circumstances will save you and your employer a lot of time, money, and effort.

As for future employers, your options are almost limitless. Every entity and or person with a website is a potential employer. Thus, big tech companies such as Apple, Amazon, Meta, Twitter, and others are not that far from your reach.
