Job description

IT System Administrators troubleshoot technical problems for employees of the company and maintain the company’s software operation (Coursera, 2022).

Education/Skill Advancement

  • Diploma or degree in CS related fields or IT (like DMIT System Administration at NAIT ☺)
  • COMPTIA+
  • The Computing Technology Industry Association (CompTIA) is a leading voice and advocate for the global information technology ecosystem.The Website provides 4 different IT certifications ranging from enrty level to expert (Kreisa, 2022)
  • Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA)
  • Cisco provides a certificate that focuses on networking fundamentals such as security, automation and network access. (Kreisa, 2022)

Skills required

    Technical skills

  • Experience in multiple operating systems (Windows, MAC OS, Linux)
  • Understanding of cyber security protocol (how to deal with phishing)
  • Device management (from employee machines to printers and such)
  • Experience in network management (Meraki, Cisco)
  • Database management
  • Programming

    Soft Skills

  • Logical troubleshooting/problem solving skills
  • Adaptability to “catastrophe” (server crashes, security breaches)
  • Collaborative mindset
  • Stress management

A day in the life of the career

  • Troubleshooting newly implemented or existing systems (email systems, networks, operating systems; this is where streams come in ) .
  • Communicating and supporting other company employees when they experience technical difficultie
  • Stay up to date on universal technology changes and brainstorm how they me be implemented
  • Dynamic and diverse responsibilities
